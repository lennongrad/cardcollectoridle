Need help? Here are some tips:
  • Earning achievements increases your level, giving you more experience to spend on cash production.
  • Buying multiple packs at once includes a discount based on how many are purchased; the more, the better.
  • Right click on an achievement icon to "pin" it, getting notifications when you make progress towards completing it.
  • Right click a card pack to view it from the other side; it's not useful, but it's fun!
  • To unlock a new mode of cash production, get to at least Level 5 in the previous one.
